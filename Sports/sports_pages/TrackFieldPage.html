Track and Field

Legends of George C. Griffin Track

Track and field is split into two distinct seasons, each with its own championship. Tech fields men’s and women’s teams in both sports. The indoor team had a strong season even without nationals. Nicole Fagens set a new indoor school record for the 3000 meters at the ACC Championships after having previously broken the school's mile record. In the world of outdoor track, Jeanine Willams had an amazing season posting a time of 12.62 seconds in the 100 meter hurdles: currently the second fastest time in the NCAA.

Even though there are no home indoor track meets on the Flats, Tech hosts two track meets in the outdoor season every year at the Dean George C. Griffin Track.
