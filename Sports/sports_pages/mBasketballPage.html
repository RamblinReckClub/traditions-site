Welcome to the Thrillerdome

Point Guard Jose Alvarado hits a clutch shot against Duke.
Source: https://ramblinwreck.com

Tech enters the 2020 season led by fifth year coach Josh Pastner and looks to build off of a promising season; they had the most ACC wins since 1996 and a huge upset win against #5 ranked Louisville. Dynamic point guard Jose Alvarado returns with fellow guard Michael DeVoe and exciting big man Moses Wright to lead an experienced and thrilling team that can compete with the best teams in the ACC.

Fittingly enough, the Jackets play at McCamish Pavilion, the “Thrillerdome,” where the team has won seven Conference Championships and has 16 NCAA Tournament appearances, two of which were Final Four appearances as recently as 2004 under Paul Hewitt. The veteran team hopes to capitalize on Pastner’s “get old and stay old” philosophy to return to the NCAA Tournament for the first time since 2010.
