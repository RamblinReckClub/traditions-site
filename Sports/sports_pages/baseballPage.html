Baseball

Witness the Danny Hall Dynasty

As temperatures begin to creep upwards and birds begin excitedly chirping on Tech’s campus, another sound begins to echo at the corner of Ferst and Fowler: the crack of aluminum against leather. Like the weather, the Yellow Jacket baseball team is warming up at historic Russ Chandler Stadium. The 2020 season seemed promising as both old and new assets to the team took the field, but the season was unfortunately cut short.

Familiar faces like Michael Guldberg and Baron Radcliffe as well as incoming freshmen Drew Compton and Stephen Reid still managed to impress in their time at the “Rusty C,” with Guldberg’s .450 batting average leading the ACC, and as a pinch-hitter, Stephen Reid putting up five home runs. On the pitching side of things, long-tenured coach Danny Hall brought in former Yankees pitching coach Danny Borrel. Returning from injury, Jonathan Hughes became the regular pitching ace and freshman Zach Maxwell impressed with 90+ MPH fastballs, 20 strikeouts, and an opposing batting average of .104.

Come enjoy a scenic Atlanta backdrop, soft pretzel with a side of mustard, and “America’s Pastime” at Russ Chandler this spring!
