Aileen Morales

Head coach of Tech's Softball Team

Aileen Morales, in her fourth year of coaching at Tech, continues the tradition of success she has pursued her entire life. One of the most legendary softball players in Tech history, Morales led Tech to the NCAA postseason four times, is the all-time team leader in games played, starts, at-bats, and stolen bases, and she was named to the All-ACC team three times.

After her amazing career, she was drafted in the second round of the National Pro Fastpitch draft and played two seasons professionally, leading her team to a title as a rookie. After that, she competed internationally for Puerto Rico and was a head coach at Young Harris and Radford. With her Tech teams improving each year she has been on campus, there is a bright future for Georgia Tech softball.
