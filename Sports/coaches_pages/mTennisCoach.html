Kenny Thorne

Head coach of the Tech Men's Tennis Team

Entering his 23rd year as head coach for Men’s Tennis, Thorne is hoping to extend his record for most career coaching victories in Tech history and lead the Jackets back to the NCAA tournament. Before his coaching tenure, Thorne actually played for the Tech tennis team back in the 80s and was a four time All-ACC selection, an All-American, and at the time had the team record for singles victories.

He then went to play professionally where he starred in doubles matches, winning two titles and achieving a career-high ranking of 67th in the world. Returning to Tech in 1997, he has brought Tech tennis back to its past glory: bringing the team to the NCAA tournament 13 times, coaching some of Tech’s best players, and even winning ACC Coach of the Year in 2017. The Jackets are in good hands with Thorne as coach!
