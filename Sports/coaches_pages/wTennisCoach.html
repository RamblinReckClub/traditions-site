Rodney Harmon

Head coach of the Tech Women's Tennis Team

Rodney Harmon has worked hard in making women’s tennis one of the finest programs in the nation during his eight years at Tech. He started off his playing career at Tennessee and SMU where he was one of the top players in the country. As a student, he advanced to the quarterfinals at the 1982 U.S. Open, and following his college career, he had a successful time in the pros, ranking 56th in the world.

After his professional career, he was head coach at the University of Miami, national coach at the United States Tennis Association (USTA), and the director of men’s tennis at USTA, including coaching the US men’s tennis team at the 2008 Olympics. His success at Tech has been phenomenal, including multiple ACC Coach of the Year awards, leading Tech to the #2 ranking in the country, and a semifinal appearance in the NCAA tournament.
