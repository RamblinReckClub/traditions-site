Grover Hinsdale

Head coach of the Tech Men's Track and Field Team

Grover Hinsdale has been a coach at Georgia Tech for the past 41 years, being the head coach of the men's track and field team for the past 27 years. He grew up in Sand Creek, Michigan, attended Ferris State for his undergraduate, and competed in the decathlon. He came to Tech to be the assistant coach under legendary coach Bobby Folkes.

His time spent at the helm of the track program has produced three Olympic gold medalists, three world champions, and 13 NCAA national champions, and Georgia Tech athletes have competed in the NCAA championship meet for the last 19 consecutive years. Grover Hinsdale has expanded and developed an incredible track team that has seen some of the greatest athletes and students in the world.
