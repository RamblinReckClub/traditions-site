It’s a cool, relaxing autumn afternoon, but the atmosphere that greets you as you enter O’Keefe Gymnasium is the complete opposite: warm and raucous. Finishing second in the ACC, the Georgia Tech volleyball team had an incredible 2019 season. After being denied the opportunity to play in the NCAA Tournament, our Yellow Jacket women went on to win the National Invitational Volleyball Championship (NIVC) with a 3-0 sweep in the final against South Dakota.

The volleyball team will be back with vengeance for a chance to participate in the 2020 NCAA Tournament. They will return with three All-ACC first team players and two All-ACC second team players. Two of the first team players, Julia Bergmann and Matti McKissock, won Freshman of the Year and Setter of the Year, respectively. Be a part of the raucous crowd this upcoming season as Michelle Collier, 2019 ACC Coach of the Year, leads the Jackets to another successful season in O’Keefe Gymnasium.
