Softball

Cheer on our Jackets in Mewborn

The Georgia Tech softball team has its fair share of legends: Jennifer Yee, Jessica Salinger, and Aileen Morales are a few. Led by Tech legend Aileen Morales, the Yellow Jackets began the 2020 season with marginal success. Emma Kauf, freshman catcher, notched a homerun in her first at bat for the Jackets and continued to smash balls out of the park with a total of six home runs during the abbreviated season. The freshmen continued to impress as Blake Neleman had the fifth best ERA in the ACC.

The future of the Yellow Jacket softball team is bright, and you have a chance to witness their continued success in the 2021 season, so be sure to make your way to Shirley Clement Mewborn Field in the spring to witness these legends in the making.
