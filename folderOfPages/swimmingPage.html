Swimming and Diving

Stars of McAuley Aquatic Center

The Yellow Jackets made a splash in this shortened season with NCAA Championship qualifiers from both swimming and diving. One small splasher but high scorer this season was junior diver Camryn Hidalgo. At zones last season, she put up a final platform score of 283.95, qualifying her for Nationals and earning a spot at the Olympic Trials. She became the fifth ever Georgia Tech diver to qualify for Nationals in the 1-meter, 3-meter, and platform. Caio Pumpitus is constantly raising the bar for the team. Entering his fourth year at Georgia Tech, Caio will probably go down as the best swimmer Tech has ever seen; he already holds the ACC record in the 100 meter breaststroke.
