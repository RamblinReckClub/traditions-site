Cross Country

The finest runners in the South

Our cross country team has been improving greatly, and this year at the South Region Championships, both the men’s and women’s teams finished in the top five. This is the third straight year the women's team has finished in the top five and the second year in a row for the men. The team will work to be a part of the 31 teams at the national NCAA meet next year.

One outstanding runner this season was Nicole Fegans who was the Yellow Jacket’s top finisher at every meet this season. She set the bar for the team this year as she earned All-ACC honors and finished 20th overall at the Conference Championships and earned All-Region honors. She has set a new pace for the women's Georgia Tech cross country team that continues to see great success and now looks forward to next season.
