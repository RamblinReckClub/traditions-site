Coach Nell Fortner’s first year was a resounding success as she led Tech to their best regular season and what would have surely been their first NCAA Tournament appearance since 2013. Who could have guessed that having the winningest National Team coach ever would lead to success?

Despite losing star Francesca Pan, Tech still has plenty of talent in Lorela Cubaj and Kierra Fletcher, along with the 12th best recruiting class in the nation. Be sure to stop by McCamish Pavilion to watch the women’s team continue the tradition of shocking top ranked ACC teams, like they did against #4 NC State, in addition to beating U[sic]GA.
